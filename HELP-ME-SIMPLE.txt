ALPHAX WEBSITE - SIMPLE HELP (read this)

You only need to do 2 things total.


THING 1 - ON YOUR COMPUTER (one time per update)
------------------------------------------------
1. Open folder:  C:\Users\intel\alphax-dezignerz-studio
2. Double-click:  START-HERE.bat
3. Wait until it says SUCCESS
4. A browser tab opens to Vercel - continue below


THING 2 - ON VERCEL WEBSITE
------------------------------------------------
1. Go to: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Sign in (same email as GitHub if you can)
3. Click your project (alphax-dezignerz-studio or similar)

   IF YOU SEE A PROJECT:
   - Click "Deployments"
   - Wait for green word "Ready" (1-3 minutes)
   - Click "Visit" button
   - That is your live website!

   IF YOU DO NOT SEE A PROJECT:
   - Click "Add New..." then "Project"
   - Find: alphax-dezignerz-studio
   - Click Import
   - Where it says Root Directory, pick:
     "alphax-dezignerz-studio (root)" with the N icon
   - Click Deploy
   - Wait for Ready, then Visit

   IF YOU SEE ERROR 404 ON OLD LINK:
   - Do NOT use old bookmarks
   - Only use the Visit button from newest Ready deployment


WHEN WEBSITE LOOKS WRONG (old version)
------------------------------------------------
1. Run START-HERE.bat again
2. On Vercel: Deployments - three dots (...) - Redeploy
3. Press Ctrl+Shift+R on the website page


STUCK? SEND SCREENSHOT OF:
------------------------------------------------
- The black window after START-HERE.bat
- OR Vercel Deployments page (showing Ready or Error)
